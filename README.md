## Projects

### 1. StudentPathGuide
AI-powered career and course guidance web app for South African high school students (Grade 10–12). Students enter their subject marks, the system calculates their APS score, recommends qualifying courses and universities, and provides career path information — without storing any personal data. Includes an AI chat assistant for personalised guidance.

**Tech:** React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Google Gemini AI  
🔗 [Live demo](https://student-path-guide-a644.vercel.app) · [GitHub](https://github.com/E-scert/StudentPathGuide)

open index.html
